Our Foundation

Management built for the owner,
not the operator.

J3T was founded on a simple premise: aircraft ownership should be transparent, efficient, and personal. We serve a select group of Part 91 owners who want expert oversight without the overhead — and without the conflict of interest — that defines most large management companies.

Our position

Most management companies make money on the spread. They mark up fuel, maintenance, and services — and the owner rarely knows. Their incentive is not to minimize your cost. It is to maximize their margin.

J3T was built on the opposite model. We are compensated by you directly. We do not take commissions from vendors. We do not mark up parts, fuel, or services. When your invoice arrives, it reflects what was actually charged.

We work exclusively with Part 91 operators. No charter. No conflict. Every recommendation we make, every vendor we engage, every dollar we spend is made with one client in mind.

The name J3T carries a personal meaning — three sons, one purpose. The business exists because ownership should be simpler, more transparent, and far less expensive than the industry has made it.

Two programs.

Program One

Oversight
Program

For owners with crew in place who need an expert advocate on the maintenance side. J3T coordinates vendors, approves work scopes, and ensures nothing is done unnecessarily or at an inflated price.

  • Maintenance planning and inspection management
  • Vendor selection, coordination, and work scope approval
  • Parts sourcing oversight and pricing review
  • Squawk tracking and deferred item management
  • Plain-language owner reporting after every event

Designed specifically for Citation X and XLS operators flying fewer than 300 hours per year — where disciplined oversight can reduce annual maintenance costs by up to 50%.

Program Two

Full Management
Program

Complete Part 91 aircraft management for owners who want everything handled. Crew, trips, maintenance, and cost optimization — all through a single relationship.

  • Everything included in the Oversight Program
  • Contract pilot and crew placement and scheduling
  • Trip coordination — flight planning, permits, handling
  • FBO and airport selection: fees, fuel, and convenience
  • Fuel program access and cost management
  • Owner representation in all vendor relationships
  • Monthly financial reporting — full transparency

Aircraft brokerage and avionics project oversight are available as separate engagements and are not included in these programs. Part 135 charter operations are not offered under any circumstance.
